# Privacy Policy for Langflix

**Last Updated:** October 16, 2025  
**Effective Date:** October 16, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Langflix ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our language learning platform at https://langflix.app (the "Service").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the Service.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information You Provide

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

- **Register for an account:** Email address, username, password
- **Use our services:** Language preferences, learning level (CEFR level A1-C2), native language
- **Contact support:** Name, email address, message content
- **Subscribe to our service:** Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)

### 1.2 Information Automatically Collected

When you access the Service, we automatically collect certain information, including:

- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, time spent on pages, videos watched, articles read, words saved, quiz results, game scores, conversation history with AI partner
- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type and version, device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Location Data:** Approximate geographic location based on IP address
- **Cookies and Tracking Technologies:** See our Cookie Policy for details

### 1.3 Learning Data

To provide personalized language learning, we collect and analyze:

- **Vocabulary:** Words you save, translation requests, word mastery levels
- **Progress Data:** Completed lessons, quiz scores, streak count, XP earned
- **AI Conversation Data:** Text and voice conversations with our AI partner (transcribed and stored)
- **Content Interactions:** Videos watched, articles read, content ratings, time spent per content
- **Assessment Data:** Placement test results, skill assessments, CEFR level progression

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:

- **Active accounts:** Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted accounts:** Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
- **Backup retention:** Deleted data removed from backups within 90 days

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting privacy@langflix.app.

Despite our efforts, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

### 6.1 GDPR Rights (European Economic Area)

If you are located in the EEA, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

- **Right to access:** Request a copy of your personal data
- **Right to rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"):** Request deletion of your data
- **Right to restrict processing:** Request limitation of how we use your data
- **Right to data portability:** Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (JSON)
- **Right to object:** Object to processing of your data for direct marketing
- **Right to withdraw consent:** Withdraw consent at any time (does not affect prior processing)
- **Right to lodge a complaint:** File a complaint with your local data protection authority

**Legal basis for processing:** We process your data based on:
- Performance of contract (providing our service)
- Legitimate interests (improving our service, analytics)
- Consent (marketing communications, optional features)

### 6.2 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- **Right to know:** Request disclosure of personal information collected, used, and shared
- **Right to delete:** Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to opt-out:** Opt-out of the "sale" of personal information (we do not sell data)
- **Right to non-discrimination:** Not receive discriminatory treatment for exercising your rights

### 6.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please:

- **Email:** privacy@langflix.app
- **In-app:** Go to Settings > Privacy > Data Export/Deletion
- **Response time:** We will respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA)

We may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request.

## 15. Specific Service Features

### 15.1 AI Voice Conversation Partner

When you use our AI Voice Conversation Partner feature:

- **Voice recordings:** Transcribed using OpenAI Whisper, stored as text
- **Audio storage:** Voice recordings are NOT stored permanently (transcribed then deleted)
- **Conversation history:** Stored to maintain context and improve learning
- **Vocabulary adaptation:** AI uses your saved vocabulary to generate appropriate responses
- **Data sharing:** Conversation text shared with OpenAI for GPT-4 processing (subject to OpenAI's privacy policy)

You can delete your conversation history at any time from Settings > AI Conversations > Clear History.

### 15.2 Content Recommendations

Our recommendation algorithm uses:

- Your CEFR level and learning progress
- Vocabulary you've saved and mastered
- Content you've engaged with (videos, articles, podcasts)
- Time spent on different content types
- User interaction patterns (anonymized and aggregated)

This data is used solely to improve your learning experience and is never sold to third parties.

### 15.3 Spaced Repetition System

Our vocabulary review system tracks:

- Words you've saved
- Review intervals based on SM-2 algorithm
- Mastery levels (0-5 scale)
- Review performance (correct/incorrect)

This data is essential for the spaced repetition feature and cannot be disabled without losing vocabulary tracking functionality.
