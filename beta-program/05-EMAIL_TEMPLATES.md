## Email Best Practices

1. **Subject lines:** Use emojis for personality, keep under 60 characters
2. **Timing:** 
   - Invites/announcements: 9-10am
   - Daily surveys: 8pm (consistent time)
   - Interviews: Send mornings, schedule anytime
3. **Tone:** Friendly, personal, honest
4. **Length:** Keep most emails under 200 words (except detailed invites)
5. **CTA:** One clear action per email
6. **Personalization:** Use [Name] tokens, reference their specific experience when possible
7. **Response time:** Reply to all email responses within 4 hours during business hours
