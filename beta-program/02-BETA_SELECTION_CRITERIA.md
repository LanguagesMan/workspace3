# Beta User Selection Criteria

## Target: 100 Beta Users

### Level Distribution (by Spanish proficiency)
- **30 users (30%)** - A1 (Complete beginners)
- **40 users (40%)** - A2-B1 (Elementary to Intermediate)
- **30 users (30%)** - B2-C2 (Upper Intermediate to Advanced)

### Device Distribution
- **50 users (50%)** - iOS (iPhone/iPad)
- **50 users (50%)** - Android

### Activity Level Requirements
Prioritize users who:
1. Are **actively learning** (daily or several times per week)
2. Have tried **multiple apps** (they understand the space)
3. Can commit to **daily check-ins**
4. Are willing to do a **30-minute interview**

### Scoring System

For each application, assign points:

#### Learning Activity (max 20 points)
- Daily practice: **20 points**
- Several times a week (3-5x): **15 points**
- Weekly (1-2x): **10 points**
- Occasionally: **5 points**
- Not currently learning: **3 points**

#### App Experience (max 15 points)
- Tried 4+ apps: **15 points**
- Tried 2-3 apps: **10 points**
- Tried 1 app: **5 points**
- Never tried any: **2 points**

#### Feedback Commitment (max 15 points)
- Daily check-ins + interview: **15 points**
- Daily check-ins OR interview: **10 points**
- Few times per week + maybe interview: **7 points**
- Just using the app: **3 points**

#### Motivation (max 10 points - subjective)
- Clear, specific motivation: **10 points**
- Vague but genuine: **6 points**
- Generic response: **3 points**

#### Total Possible: 60 points

### Selection Process

1. **First Pass: Filter by commitment**
   - Must score at least **7 points** in Feedback Commitment
   - We need engaged users who will actually help

2. **Second Pass: Balance the mix**
   - Ensure level distribution (30/40/30)
   - Ensure device distribution (50/50)
   - Select highest-scoring applicants within each category

3. **Third Pass: Edge cases**
   - Particularly compelling stories?
   - Diversity of use cases (travel, work, heritage, etc.)
   - Geographic diversity (for timezone coverage)

### Waitlist Management

- Keep a **waitlist of 50 users** (ranked by score)
- If beta users drop out, invite from waitlist
- Send waitlist email to non-selected applicants

### Red Flags (Automatic Rejection)

- Can't commit to any feedback
- Inconsistent answers (e.g., "never tried apps" but lists 5 apps)
- Spammy or bot-like responses
- No clear reason for wanting to learn Spanish

### Timeline

- **Day 8-10:** Post recruitment messages, collect applications
- **Day 11:** First selection round (select top 60)
- **Day 12:** Second selection round (select remaining 40)
- **Day 13:** Send acceptance emails
- **Day 14:** Follow up with non-responders, pull from waitlist if needed
- **Day 15:** Launch beta with confirmed 100 users
