# Langflix Beta Application Form

## Google Form Setup Instructions

1. Go to Google Forms (forms.google.com)
2. Create new form titled: "Langflix Beta Program Application"
3. Add the following questions:

---

## Form Questions

### Question 1: What's your Spanish level?
**Type:** Multiple choice (required)

Options:
- Complete beginner (A1) - I know basic words
- Elementary (A2) - I can have simple conversations
- Intermediate (B1) - I can discuss familiar topics
- Upper Intermediate (B2) - I can express myself fluently
- Advanced (C1-C2) - I'm near native level

---

### Question 2: Why do you want to learn Spanish?
**Type:** Short answer (required)

Placeholder: "Tell us your motivation..."

---

### Question 3: What language learning apps have you tried?
**Type:** Checkboxes (required)

Options:
- Duolingo
- Babbel
- Rosetta Stone
- Memrise
- Busuu
- Pimsleur
- Anki
- None
- Other: ___

---

### Question 4: What device will you primarily use?
**Type:** Multiple choice (required)

Options:
- iPhone (iOS)
- iPad (iOS)
- Android Phone
- Android Tablet
- Desktop/Web Browser

---

### Question 5: How actively are you currently learning Spanish?
**Type:** Multiple choice (required)

Options:
- Daily (I practice every day)
- Several times a week (3-5x per week)
- Weekly (1-2x per week)
- Occasionally (less than once a week)
- Not currently learning (but want to restart)

---

### Question 6: Email Address
**Type:** Email (required)

Placeholder: "your.email@example.com"

---

### Question 7: Are you willing to provide daily feedback during the 2-week beta?
**Type:** Multiple choice (required)

Options:
- Yes, I can commit to daily check-ins
- Yes, but only a few times per week
- Maybe, depending on my schedule
- No, I prefer to just use the app

---

### Question 8: Would you be available for a 30-minute user interview?
**Type:** Multiple choice (required)

Options:
- Yes, I'd love to help!
- Maybe, if timing works
- No, but I'm happy to provide written feedback

---

### Question 9: How did you hear about Langflix?
**Type:** Multiple choice (required)

Options:
- Reddit (r/Spanish or r/languagelearning)
- Discord server
- Facebook group
- Friend or family
- Social media (Twitter, Instagram, etc.)
- Other: ___

---

### Question 10: Anything else we should know?
**Type:** Paragraph (optional)

Placeholder: "Any additional context, special requirements, or questions?"
