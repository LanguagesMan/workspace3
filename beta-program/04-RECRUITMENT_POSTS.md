## Tips for Posting

1. **Timing matters**
   - Reddit: Post early morning (7-9am) or evening (7-9pm) EST
   - Discord: Any time, but be ready to respond
   - Facebook: Midday (12-2pm) gets most engagement
   - Twitter: Multiple times (morning, noon, evening)

2. **Respond quickly**
   - Answer questions within 15 minutes if possible
   - Be friendly and approachable
   - Thank people for applying

3. **Track which posts work best**
   - Ask in the Google Form: "How did you hear about Langflix?"
   - Double down on channels that convert

4. **Don't spam**
   - One post per subreddit/group
   - Follow community rules
   - Provide value, don't just ask for help
