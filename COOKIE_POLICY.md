# Cookie Policy for Langflix

**Last Updated:** October 16, 2025  
**Effective Date:** October 16, 2025

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. Cookies help websites remember your preferences, track your activity, and improve your experience.

## How Langflix Uses Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies (web beacons, pixels, local storage) to:

1. **Essential Functions:** Keep you logged in, remember your settings
2. **Performance:** Understand how you use our Service to improve it
3. **Personalization:** Recommend content based on your level and interests
4. **Security:** Protect against fraudulent activity
5. **Analytics:** Measure user engagement and feature usage

## How to Control Cookies

### Option 1: In-App Cookie Settings

1. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy** > **Cookie Preferences**
2. Toggle categories on/off:
   - ✅ Essential (always on)
   - ⚪ Functional
   - ⚪ Analytics
   - ⚪ Performance
3. Click **Save Preferences**

### Option 2: Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

**Google Chrome:**
1. Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
2. Choose "Block third-party cookies" or "Block all cookies"
3. [Chrome Cookie Settings](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)

**Mozilla Firefox:**
1. Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
2. Choose "Delete cookies and site data when Firefox is closed"
3. [Firefox Cookie Settings](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)

**Safari:**
1. Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
2. Choose "Block all cookies"
3. [Safari Cookie Settings](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-and-website-data-sfri11471/mac)

**Microsoft Edge:**
1. Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data
2. Toggle "Block third-party cookies"
3. [Edge Cookie Settings](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

**Mobile Browsers:**
- **iOS Safari:** Settings > Safari > Block All Cookies
- **Android Chrome:** Chrome > Settings > Site settings > Cookies

**Warning:** Blocking essential cookies will prevent you from logging in and using key features.

### Option 3: Third-Party Opt-Outs

Opt out of specific third-party tracking:

- **Mixpanel:** [Mixpanel Opt-Out](https://mixpanel.com/optout)
- **Google Analytics:** [Google Analytics Opt-Out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- **All advertising cookies:** [Your Online Choices](http://www.youronlinechoices.com/) (EU)
- **All advertising cookies:** [NAI Opt-Out](http://optout.networkadvertising.org/) (US)

### Option 4: Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers support "Do Not Track" signals:
- Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Send a "Do Not Track" request
- Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Send websites a "Do Not Track" signal

**Note:** We currently do not respond to DNT signals, but we respect opt-out preferences set in our app.

## Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit Langflix, you'll see a cookie consent banner with these options:

1. **Accept All:** Allows all cookies (essential, functional, analytics, performance)
2. **Reject Non-Essential:** Only essential cookies (you can still use the Service)
3. **Customize:** Choose which cookie categories to allow

Your choice is stored in the cookie `cookie_consent` (duration: 1 year). You can change your preferences anytime in Settings.

### If You Disable Essential Cookies:
- ❌ Cannot log in or stay logged in
- ❌ Cannot save preferences
- ❌ Security features won't work
- ❌ Service will not function properly

### If You Disable Functional Cookies:
- ⚠️ Preferences not remembered (language, volume, etc.)
- ⚠️ Must set preferences each visit
- ✅ Core features still work

### If You Disable Analytics Cookies:
- ⚠️ We can't measure how you use the Service
- ⚠️ Recommendations may be less accurate
- ✅ All features still work normally

### If You Disable Performance Cookies:
- ⚠️ We can't detect and fix errors as quickly
- ⚠️ Performance issues may take longer to resolve
- ✅ Service works normally

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do cookies contain personal information?

A: Most cookies contain only an ID number. Personal information (like your email) is stored in our database, not in cookies. Cookies simply link your browser to your account.

### Q: Can I use Langflix without cookies?

A: You need essential cookies to use Langflix. Non-essential cookies are optional.

### Q: Do you sell cookie data to third parties?

A: **No.** We never sell your personal information or cookie data. See our [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md) for details.

### Q: How do I delete existing cookies?

A: 
1. **In-app:** Settings > Privacy > Clear Cookies
2. **Browser:** See "How to Control Cookies" section above

### Q: Will my cookie choices sync across devices?

A: If you're logged in, your cookie preferences sync across devices. If logged out, preferences are device-specific.

### Q: Do you use cookies for advertising?

A: Currently, no. If we add advertising in the future, we will:
- Update this Cookie Policy
- Obtain your consent for advertising cookies
- Allow you to opt out

### Q: What happens if I reject all non-essential cookies?

A: You can still use all core features of Langflix:
- ✅ Watch videos and read articles
- ✅ Save vocabulary
- ✅ Take quizzes and play games
- ✅ Use AI conversation partner
- ⚠️ Preferences won't be saved between sessions
- ⚠️ Recommendations may be less accurate
