### Video Feed

**How to use:**

## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Success

### Daily Routine

**Recommended daily routine (30 minutes):**

1. **Warm-up (5 min)** - Review vocabulary flashcards
2. **Active learning (15 min)** - Watch 2-3 videos or read an article
3. **Practice (5 min)** - Complete daily quiz
4. **Conversation (5 min)** - Chat with AI tutor

### Learning Strategies

**For Beginners:**
- Start with videos (more engaging)
- Use dual subtitles
- Save every new word
- Review vocabulary daily

**For Intermediate:**
- Mix videos and articles
- Try target language subtitles only
- Focus on phrases, not single words
- Practice with AI chat daily

**For Advanced:**
- Read news articles daily
- Watch without subtitles first
- Save idioms and expressions
- Have longer AI conversations

### Staying Motivated

- **Set realistic goals** - Start with 10 min/day
- **Join challenges** - Compete with friends
- **Track progress** - Watch your XP grow
- **Celebrate wins** - Acknowledge achievements
- **Mix it up** - Vary between videos, articles, music
