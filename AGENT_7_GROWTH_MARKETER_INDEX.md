## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips from Successful Launches

### Product Hunt

âœ… **Do:**
- Launch Tuesday or Wednesday (best traffic)
- Respond to EVERY comment within 10 minutes
- Rally supporters BEFORE launch (not after)
- Share genuine story in maker comment
- Use high-quality screenshots
- Include demo video

âŒ **Don't:**
- Launch Friday or Monday (low traffic)
- Ignore comments (kills ranking)
- Be overly salesy (community hates it)
- Use AI-generated responses (people can tell)
- Forget to thank supporters

### Social Media

âœ… **Do:**
- Post at peak times (9am, 1pm, 7pm local)
- Use storytelling (not features)
- Include visual content (video > image > text)
- Engage with comments immediately
- Use relevant hashtags (3-5 max)

âŒ **Don't:**
- Post only links (no context)
- Ignore negative comments
- Over-hashtag (looks spammy)
- Post same content to all platforms
- Forget to include CTA

### Viral Growth

âœ… **Do:**
- Make sharing effortless (one-click)
- Reward both referrer and friend
- Trigger shares at moment of delight
- Track everything obsessively
- Test different incentives

âŒ **Don't:**
- Make rewards too small (not worth it)
- Make sharing complicated (kills conversion)
- Forget to follow up with referrers
- Ignore which channels work best
- Stop after initial setup
