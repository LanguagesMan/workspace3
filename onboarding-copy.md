## ðŸŽ¨ Visual Design Notes

### Colors
- Primary: Purple (#7C3AED)
- Secondary: Blue (#3B82F6)
- Success: Green (#10B981)
- Background: White / Light Gray (#F9FAFB)

### Typography
- Headlines: Bold, 28-32px
- Subheadlines: Medium, 18-20px
- Body: Regular, 16px
- Buttons: Bold, 16-18px

### Animations
- Screen transitions: Slide left/right (300ms)
- Button press: Scale down slightly + haptic feedback
- Success: Confetti animation (1 second)
- Loading: Pulse or spinner (subtle)

### Accessibility
- High contrast text
- Large touch targets (min 44x44px)
- Screen reader compatible
- Skip button always visible
- Support for reduce motion preference

## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Implementation

1. **Keep it fast:** Every second counts. Aim for <2s per screen transition.

2. **Make it skippable:** Power users hate tutorials. Let them skip but make it easy to access help later.

3. **Personalize instantly:** Use their level selection to customize everything immediately.

4. **Celebrate small wins:** First word tapped, first word saved, first video done - celebrate everything.

5. **Don't ask for too much:** No email, no notifications, no settings until AFTER first video.

6. **Test on real devices:** Animations that work on Figma may lag on Android.

7. **Analytics everywhere:** Track every click, every drop-off, every completion.

8. **Iterate weekly:** Onboarding is never done. Always optimize.
