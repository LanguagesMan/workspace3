**Answer:** Yes! No contracts, no commitments. Cancel from your account settings in 30 seconds.

### Question 4: What makes Langflix different from Duolingo?
**Answer:** Duolingo uses artificial exercises. Langflix uses real videos. Instead of translating "The turtle eats apples," you're learning from actual Spanish content. Plus, TikTok-style UX makes it addictive.

**Answer:** Nope! Complete beginners start with A1 videos (simple phrases, common words). You'll be surprised how fast you pick it up.
